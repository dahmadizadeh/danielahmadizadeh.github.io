Hi, I’m Daniel — I’m currently growing Crustdata, a Y Combinator startup building the Google for AI agents. In 2025, we’ve grown from $700K to $5M ARR (as of October 30).

I also teach entrepreneurship at Columbia University and Stanford University. Over the last 10+ years I've been the CEO of two Y Combinator–backed startups.

I have helped lead three companies to over $1M+ ARR, and my work has been recognized in publications like WSJ, The Today Show, and NPR. I’ve spoken at Harvard, Princeton, and TED (where I was a TED Resident).

My dream is to change the way entrepreneurship is taught globally — shifting it from theoretical to practical. Over three years I’ve taught 500+ students, helping them leave the classroom more confident in their next steps.

My framework, Start With One, focuses on solving a real problem for one real person, as a microcosm of an entire market.

Start With One has guided founders to launch new ventures and measure their impact. See testimonials here. Join my weekly office hours for live advice.
